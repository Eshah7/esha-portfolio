Loblaw Digital — PC Optimum App

Experimentation, Offer Design, Stakeholder Buy-in
Role
Data Analyst Intern
Timeline
Jan 2024 – Apr 2024
Team
Digital Member Growth

Summary

Conducted statistical analysis on customer behaviour to create new offers and increase weekly active users on the PC Optimum app. Projects included EDA in BigQuery, experiment design, weekly stakeholder readouts, and the launch of a new offer for more PC Optimum points.

Skills

  • A/B testing & hypothesis-driven experiments
  • Exploratory data analysis in BigQuery and Excel
  • Offer design & specification docs
  • Cross-functional collaboration & senior leadership readouts

Results

  • Birthday Offer: launched; +4% in Weekly Active Users in the treatment group.
  • Better Deals for New Members: rebalanced offer bundles to improve early offer redemption by +3%.
  • Improving Onboarding Effectiveness: better understood the ~+11% increase new members remaining active for 4+ weeks to enhance marketing strategy.
  • Loyalty Team Dashboard: shared KPI updates through a redesigned dashboard to the entire Loyalty department on weekly calls.

A Special Birthday Offer for PC Optimum Members

Project Goal

Increase weekly active users and repeat visits by celebrating members’ birthdays with targeted incentives such as bonus points.

Approach

  1. EDA in BigQuery to size eligible audience for birthday offers with different incentives and determine the control group.
  2. Better understand the impact of the offer by calculating the lift and stickiness of the weekly active users through in-week and post 4 week analysis.
  3. Developed a narrative to pitch the offer to cross-functional teams and obtain senior leadership approval.

Outcomes

  • Obtained the most optimal amount of points to offer to PC Optimum members.
  • Calculated a +4% increase in the number of weekly active users in comparsion to the control group!

Learnings

  • The importance of having a control group to understand the impact of your tests.
  • How to develop a story from your data to gain stakeholder buy-in.

New Member Offer Sets

Project Goal

Improve first-month redemption and points earned for new members.

Approach

  1. Utilized BigQuery to calculate the offer redemption % for every offer in the offer sets.
  2. Understood the offer redemption % from user segmentation perspective (user activity and geographical).
  3. Rebalanced the offer sets to ensure that new members have at least one low-friction offer (backed by redemption %) to every week for their first month.
  4. Redesigned the onboarding customer survey with questions to personalize offers (ex. no meat offers for a vegeterian

Outcomes

  • ~+3% redemption in month one; higher points velocity.

Learnings

  • Early success compounds—ensure at least one low-friction win.

Engagement Cohort Analysis

Project Goal

Identify where users drop and which mechanics correlate with retention.

Approach

  • Cohorted by signup channel & first-week actions; profiled high-LTV patterns.
  • Surfaced mechanics for future tests (variety, novelty, progression).

Outcomes

  • Prioritized pipeline of experiments feeding Birthday & Onboarding work.

Learnings

  • Offer novelty decays—rotate mechanics to avoid diminishing returns.
